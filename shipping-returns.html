Shipping & Returns

Clear policies support confident purchases.

Keep this page concise, accurate, and aligned with your real fulfillment process.

Shipping

  • Standard shipping: 3 to 5 business days.
  • Express shipping: 1 to 2 business days.
  • Order processing generally occurs within 1 business day.
  • Shipping timelines may vary during peak periods.

Returns

  • Eligible returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused and returned in original condition.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.

Damaged or incorrect items

Please contact support within 5 days of delivery if an item arrives damaged or does not match the order. Return handling should align with your actual operations and carrier policies.
